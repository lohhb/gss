Item requested: IFUNT200 card

Part Code:  R33N800003500  (Single port, SKP457)


 

Symptom

No LED status on IFUN Unit behind handler. Verified with Loader.exe, with " Verify Error, Address oh, D3h, F3h".


 

Remarks

IFUNT200 card only have one port.

IFUNT100B, code: R35N700012300

IFUNT200, code: R35N800003500  (Single port, SKP457)


 

Replace the IFUNT100B / IFUNT200 board by the same procedures as the MIB.

The jumpers do not need to be set.

The IFUN100B have a built-in CPU and can be operated stand alone. Therefore, it is

required to activate and download utility software, “Loader.exe”.

“Loader.exe” can be found in the directory: “C£º\NS8000\Tool\”.
