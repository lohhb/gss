Error 197   Framing error of encoder data

Power Cycle Required

When this error occurs, the power should be turned off once and on again to recover from the error.

Causes

1. This error is shown after replacing a motor.

2. Strong external noise source exists.

3. Other than above.

Remedies

1. Eliminate external noise source.

2. Take the following procedures:

a.     Check inserted condition of MPU board.

b.    Replace MPU board.

c.     Replace motor.
